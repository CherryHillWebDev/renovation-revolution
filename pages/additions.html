Home Additions That Grow With Your Family

Running out of space doesn't mean you need to move. At Remodeling Revolution, we specialize in creating home additions that expand your living area while maintaining the character and style of your existing home.

Whether you need an extra bedroom, a larger kitchen, a sunroom, or a complete second-story addition, our team has the expertise to make it happen. We handle everything from initial design and permits to foundation work, framing, and finishing touches.

With 15 years of experience serving Moorestown, Mt. Laurel, Cherry Hill, Haddon Township, and Maple Shade, we understand local building codes and architectural styles. We ensure your addition looks like it was always part of your home, not an afterthought.

Features (What's Included)

Room Additions

Create additional bedrooms, bathrooms, family rooms, or dining spaces. We design additions that flow naturally with your existing floor plan and match your home's aesthetic.

Second-Story Additions

Maximize your property's footprint by building up instead of out. Perfect for adding master suites, home offices, or extra bedrooms without sacrificing yard space.

Sunrooms & Screened Porches

Bring the outdoors in with beautiful sunrooms or enjoy fresh air in screened porches. Custom-designed spaces that extend your living area and provide year-round enjoyment.

Garage Additions

Add a new garage or expand your existing one. We handle everything from foundation to electrical, ensuring your vehicles and storage needs are met.

In-Law Suites

Create comfortable, private living spaces for extended family. Complete with bedrooms, bathrooms, and kitchenettes designed for independent living.

Structural & Foundation Work

Expert handling of all structural requirements including foundations, load-bearing walls, roof integration, and proper drainage to ensure lasting stability.
